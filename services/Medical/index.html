Bio Bracelet

Features:

  • Monitors Basic Vital Signs of Patients and Staff (until vaccine is available)
  • Provides “Raw” or “Pre-processed” Data under control of the CMS
  • Fast, Automatic Connection to Network when Turned On
  • Optional Emergency Call Button/Nurse Call Button (bedpan/not feeling well) Monitoring of Battery Reserve (7 Day Nominal Charge)
  • Uses CELLULAR/Wi-Fi to Transmit Patient Data to Central Monitoring System

Central Monitoring

Features:

  • Tracks each Patient Individually
  • Trends Vital Signs and Generates Warnings on Negative Trends
  • Thresholds Vital Signs and Alarms on Exceeded Thresholds (REDUCES ALARM FATIGUE)
  • Allows Instant Access and Display of Current and Past Patient Data
  • Provides Automatic Notification of Scheduled Patient Medication
  • Logs and Archives all Patient Data RECORD/PLAYBACK for Incident Investigation
